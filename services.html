Project Loan

Project finance is long-term funding for infrastructure or industrial projects, based on projected cash flows rather than the sponsors’ balance sheets. These are usually non-recourse loans secured by project assets and repaid entirely from project cash flow. The financing is backed by all project assets, including revenue-generating contracts, with viability supported through financial modeling.

Key features:

  • Capital Intensive Financing Scheme
  • Risk Allocation
  • Zero or Limited Recourse Financing Solution
  • Loan Repayment With Project Cash Flow
  • Multiple Participants Applicable
  • Better Tax Treatment
